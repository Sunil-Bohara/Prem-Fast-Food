Prem Fast Food

Prem Fast Food is a fast food store based in Ambala, Haryana, India. Prem Fast Food was established in 1990 in Mahesh Nagar of Ambala Cantt. Prem Fast Food was found by Prakash K.C. and father of Prem K.C. Ever since then Prem Fast Food has made an immense growth. Prem Fast Food has become a very popular fast food store in Mahesh Nagar, Ambala Cantt. People come here to enjoy tasty fast food in evening.Prem Fast Food also sells the raw material of fast food items to other vendors.

Prem K.C.

Prem K.C. is the owner of Prem Fast Food store. He has experience of 15 years in this industry. He started his journey in this store with his father. Soon he take over to the store and now has successfully completed 15 years in this store. Prem K.C. is also associated with the marketing department of DXN India. He is a very determind person and knows how to run a successfull fast food store.
